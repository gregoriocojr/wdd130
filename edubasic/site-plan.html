Overview

Purpose

The website will contain general information that will help teachers and parent devolop basic literacy and numeracy among grade school children. The home page of the website will contain general status of reading and numeracy levels in our city. It will have a hero image of children in schools. A child page will contain strategies and information that will help children improve their literacy skills.

Audience

Teachers of grade school children and parents that have children still learning basic literacy and numeracy.

Content

Home page

Welcome to EduBasic: Empowering Educators and Parents for Grade School Literacy and Numeracy!

Reading Landscape in the City

On the home page of EduBasic, visitors can gain valuable insights into the reading landscape within our city. By presenting up-to-date data and analysis, EduBasic aims to provide educators and parents with a clear understanding of the current state of literacy levels among grade school children. This information serves as a starting point for identifying areas of improvement and setting realistic goals for young learners.

Benefits of EduBasic for the Audiences

At EduBasic, we understand the immense impact that teachers and parents have on a child's educational journey. Our website is designed to provide valuable benefits to both these audiences:

1. For Educators:

- Access to research-based practices and instructional techniques to enhance teaching strategies.
- A wealth of lesson plans, activities, and resources to support literacy and numeracy instruction.
- Opportunities to collaborate and share insights with a community of like-minded educators.
- Insights into the current reading landscape in our city, enabling targeted interventions.

2. For Parents:

- Practical strategies and activities to engage children in reading and numeracy at home.
Guidance on how to support and reinforce classroom learning effectively.
Recommended reading lists and resources to enhance children's language skills.
Insights into the current reading landscape in our city, fostering informed decisions.

Our website aims to empower both educators and parents, enabling them to create an engaging and effective learning environment for grade school children.
